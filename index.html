Fundra

Fundra is a modern personal finance platform designed to make budgeting, saving, and tracking money simple and intuitive. The app helps users stay organized, monitor spending habits, and build stronger financial habits with clear insights and smart automation.

Available on iOS Android macOS Web

Future Projects

Fundra Pro

Fundra Pro will extend the Fundra platform with Plaid integration allowing users to securely link their bank accounts and track savings goals in real time.

Future plans also include collaborative savings features where friends or family can work together toward a shared financial goal.

Snapolytics

Snapolytics is an analytics tool designed to take screenshots and automatically extract useful data. The app will convert visual information into structured reports and dashboards using intelligent data logic.
