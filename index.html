Products & Initiatives

Fundra

Fundra is a modern personal finance platform designed to make budgeting, saving, and tracking money simple and intuitive. The app helps users stay organized, monitor spending habits, and build stronger financial habits with clear insights and smart automation.

Available on iOS Android macOS
