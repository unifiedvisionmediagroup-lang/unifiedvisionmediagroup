About Unified Vision Media Group

Unified Vision Media Group is a multi-division company built to develop modern software, creative media, publishing ventures, and independent music properties under one scalable brand. The company is designed to support digital products, original content, professional services, intellectual property ownership, and long-term business growth across multiple industries.

Founder, CEO & Developer

Leadership and vision behind Unified Vision Media Group.

Ralph A. Caballero

Founder, CEO, Developer, Audio Engineer, Producer & Recording Artist

Ralph A. Caballero is the Founder and CEO of Unified Vision Media Group, where he leads the company’s vision across software, media, publishing, and music ventures. As a developer, entrepreneur, and creative executive, he is building a scalable multi-division company designed to unify digital products, intellectual property, and original content under one modern brand.

With over 20 years of experience in audio engineering and running recording studios, Ralph brings deep industry knowledge to the company’s music and production divisions. His background includes work with multiple professional digital audio workstations, including Pro Tools, Reaper, Propellerhead Reason, Nuendo, and other recording and production systems used in professional studio environments.

In addition to his technical studio background, Ralph is also a recording artist, songwriter, and producer with firsthand experience in the creative process from concept to release. He combines that experience with his growing work in Flutter app development and digital product strategy, positioning Unified Vision Media Group as both a creative and technology-driven company with strong long-term potential.
