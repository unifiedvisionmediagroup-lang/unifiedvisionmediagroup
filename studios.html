About the Studio

UNI FX Studios is built for independent artists, serious creators, and developing talent looking for a professional recording environment with modern tools and a strong creative workflow. The studio supports vocal tracking, mixing, mastering, and artist development with a focus on polished, release-ready sound.

DAWs & Production Tools

Pro Tools, Reaper, Auto-Tune, Melodyne, Revoice Pro, Vocalign, iZotope Ozone, and a large suite of professional mixing, mastering, and vocal plugins.

Studio Gear

WA-87 microphone, Icon Pro Audio P1-M DAW control surface, JBL studio monitors, and a professional workflow built for recording, editing, mixing, and mastering.
