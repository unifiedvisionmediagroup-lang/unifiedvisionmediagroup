About the Division

UNI FX Vision is built for artists, creators, brands, and clients who want clean, professional visuals backed by strong production value. The division is designed to support music-driven visuals, single-camera podcast production, photography sessions, short film projects, and memorable event coverage with a polished creative approach.

Production Tools

Sony A6700, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, Meld Studio, professional lighting, green screen capability, and a growing production workflow built for both recorded and live content.

Creative Direction

UNI FX Vision focuses on strong visuals, clean editing, basic color correction, lighting control, and creative execution that helps independent artists and clients present themselves professionally.

Service Pricing

Focused service pricing for core visual production work. Basic color correction is included where applicable for clean, natural visuals.
